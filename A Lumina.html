Where It All Began

Founded in 2008 by Isabella Laurent, Lumina began as a small boutique in the heart of Paris. Isabella's vision was simple yet profound: to create jewelry that captures the interplay of light and form, resulting in pieces that tell stories and become heirlooms.

What started as a single workshop with three artisans has grown into an internationally recognized brand, but our commitment to handcrafted excellence remains unchanged. Each piece continues to be created with the same attention to detail and passion that defined our beginnings.

Today, Lumina operates from our atelier in New York, where our team of master jewelers continues Isabella's legacy, blending traditional techniques with contemporary design to create jewelry that stands the test of time.

The Art of Craftsmanship

At Lumina, jewelry making is more than a processit's an art form passed down through generations of master artisans. Each piece undergoes 15 distinct stages of creation, from initial sketch to final polish.

Ethically Sourced Materials: We use only conflict-free diamonds and responsibly mined precious metals.

Handcrafted Precision: Every piece is individually crafted by our master jewelers, with attention to the smallest details.

Quality Assurance: Each piece undergoes rigorous quality checks before it reaches our customers.

Sustainable Practices: We recycle precious metals and use eco-friendly packaging to minimize our environmental footprint.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in Lumina's history

2008

Lumina Is Founded

Isabella Laurent opens the first Lumina boutique in Paris with a vision to create timeless jewelry that captures light and tells stories.

2012

First International Recognition

Lumina receives the prestigious "Artisans of Excellence" award at the Paris Jewelry Week, marking our entry into the international jewelry scene.

2015

New York Atelier

We establish our flagship atelier in New York City, bringing European craftsmanship to the American market and expanding our team of master jewelers.

2018

Sustainability Initiative

Lumina commits to 100% ethical sourcing and launches our recycling program for precious metals, setting new industry standards for sustainability.

2020

Digital Expansion

We launch our e-commerce platform, making Lumina jewelry accessible worldwide while maintaining our commitment to personalized service.

2023

15th Anniversary Collection

To celebrate 15 years of craftsmanship, we launch our Heritage Collection featuring reimagined versions of our most iconic early designs.
